Team Smart State Deliverables

This page details the deliverables that are due from the team. This schedule includes assignments due for the course and tasks that have been self-assigned by the team. Some of the assignments are links to our documents or presentations. Please hover over the assignment names to follow a link to one of our deliverables.

Schedule Due Date Description
Team Inventory 9/24/19 The team inventory is a document that contains information about each team member. This includes their professional experience, skills, and personal interests.

Mini-Intro Presentation 9/30/19 This presentation is a five minute introduction to our sponsor, the problem in with thier system, and our solution.

Design Review 11/16/19 Presentation that will provde details regarding the design process that has been developed for the project.

Final Requirements Specification 12/9/19 Comprehensive document containing all requirements specified by the client.
